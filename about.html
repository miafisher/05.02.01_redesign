About us

We proudly welcome you to Tim's cookies sweet universe. Tim's cookies are a culturally enhanced bakery with focus on taste, quality and only the best ingredients. Tim's cookies were established in Copenhagen on a cold November morning back in 1985 by the Danish-American Tim Pape, who was born in Hollywood, California.

It all started with a longing for his home country's Chocolate Chip Cookies, so Tim decided to open his own small bakery on Nørrebrogade 42, where it was all about sharing Tim's passion for delicious cookies with the outside world. Tim's Cookies quickly became a success, and later the range was expanded based on Tim's sweet tooth. Over the past 30 years, Tim's has sent millions of cookies, brownies, banana cakes and rum truffles over the counter.

Tim's has many exclusive dealers around Denmark and in Norway. It is proudly sold to Nemlig.com, Interflora, Salling, BC Catering, 7Eleven and many more.

Ultimately, it's about sharing Tim's passion and love for good ingredients, by creating the perfect combination of taste and quality so you can always get a little sweet something.
